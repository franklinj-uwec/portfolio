About

John Franklin is Currently in his third year of studying journalism at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire. During his time there, he became an Eagle Scout with Bayport, Minnesota's troop 113, was an editor-in-chief for the Pony Express Newspaper, and played percussion for the band.

In his free time, John likes to run, play video games, watch automotive racing, and hang out with friends.
