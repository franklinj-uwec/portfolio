About

John Franklin is Currently in his third year of studying journalism at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire. John was born and raised in Lake Elmo, Minnesota, where he graduated from Stillwater Area High School in 2020. During his time there, he became an Eagle Scout with Bayport, Minnesota's troop 113, was an editor-in-chief for the Pony Express Newspaper, and played percussion for the band.

In his free time, John likes to run, play video games, watch automotive racing, and hang out with friends.
